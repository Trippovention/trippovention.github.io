General Refund Conditions

  • All refund requests must be made in writing via email to our operations or sales team.
  • Refunds (if applicable) will be processed only after receiving the same from the respective supplier (airline, hotel, cruise, insurance company, or any other third party).
  • The processing time for refunds is typically 15‚Äì45 working days from the date we receive the supplier's credit.

  • Certain booking charges, service fees, or payment gateway charges may be non-refundable, even in case of cancellation.
  • Trippovention does not bear responsibility for supplier delays in processing refunds.

Flight Bookings

Domestic & International

  • Airline tickets are subject to the individual airline's refund and cancellation policy.
  • Refunds depend on the fare type (Refundable / Non-Refundable / Partially Refundable) and cancellation timeline.
  • Once a ticket is issued, any cancellation or modification will incur applicable airline penalties and Trippovention service charges.
  • In cases of flight rescheduling, delays, or operational cancellations by the airline, the refund or rebooking option will be processed as per the airline's final decision.
  • Refunds will be made only after the airline confirms and transfers the applicable amount to Trippovention.

Hotel Bookings

Domestic & International

  • Each hotel has its own cancellation and refund policy, which will apply to your booking.
  • Refund eligibility depends on the check-in date and cancellation timeline shared during booking.
  • Non-refundable rates or "no-show" bookings are not eligible for refunds.
  • If a hotel is unable to provide the booked service due to operational issues, Trippovention will coordinate for a full or partial refund, as per the hotel's approval.

Holiday Packages

  • Refunds for holiday packages depend on the cancellation date, the type of services booked, and supplier rules.
  • Airfare, hotel bookings, and activity costs included in a package follow their individual supplier cancellation policies.
  • Once travel has commenced, no partial refunds will be applicable for unutilized services.
  • In the event of force majeure (e.g., natural calamities, government restrictions, or pandemic-related disruptions), refund eligibility will depend on supplier discretion and applicable conditions.

Visa Services

  • Visa fees and service charges are strictly non-refundable, regardless of the outcome of the visa application.
  • Embassy or consulate decisions are beyond Trippovention's control, and no refund will be issued for visa rejections, delays, or withdrawals.
  • If a visa application is cancelled before submission to the embassy, only a partial refund (if applicable) may be processed after deducting administrative costs.
Important: Visa decisions are at the sole discretion of the respective embassy or immigration authority.

Cruise Bookings

  • Cruise deposits and payments are subject to the cruise line's own refund and cancellation policy.
  • Refund timelines and deductions depend on how close the cancellation is to the sailing date.
  • In some cases, cruise operators may issue future cruise credits instead of cash refunds, as per their global policy.
  • Trippovention acts as a facilitator and will process refunds only after receiving the refund amount or credit confirmation from the cruise company.

Travel Insurance

  • Once issued, travel insurance policies cannot be refunded, transferred, or cancelled, as per the terms of the respective insurance provider.
  • In cases of claim rejection or policy disputes, customers must contact the insurance company directly.
  • Trippovention's role is limited to policy issuance and does not include refund or claim handling on behalf of insurers.
Note: All insurance claims must be processed directly with the insurance provider.

Force Majeure Refunds
