Refund Policy

Flexible cancellation terms designed for your peace of mind

At Trippovention, we understand that plans can change. Our refund policy is designed to offer you flexibility while maintaining fairness for all parties involved.

Note: Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days to the original payment method.

Cancellation Policy

Understanding our refund structure based on cancellation timing

✅

Full Refund

30+ Days Before Departure

Cancel more than 30 days before your scheduled departure date and receive a full refund of the amount paid.

⚠️

Partial Refund

15-30 Days Before Departure

Cancellations within this window receive a 50% refund of the booking amount.

❌

No Refund

Less than 15 Days Before

Unfortunately, we cannot offer refunds for cancellations made less than 15 days before departure.

🔄 Booking Modifications

📅 Date Changes

You can change your trip dates up to 15 days before departure. Any fare differences or additional fees will apply.

🗺️ Destination Changes

Destination changes are subject to availability and applicable fare differences. Contact our team for assistance.

🚫 Non-Refundable Bookings

Certain bookings are marked as non-refundable at the time of purchase:

  • Special promotions and flash sales
  • Last-minute deals
  • Specific travel packages

Important: The 'Cancel Booking' option will not be available for non-refundable reservations.

⚕️ Medical Emergencies

In case of a medical emergency, please provide a medical certificate. We will review your request for a refund or rescheduling on a case-by-case basis.

Our priority is your wellbeing, and we strive to accommodate genuine emergencies with compassion and flexibility.

🚧 Travel Restrictions

If government-imposed travel restrictions affect your trip, we will offer options for rescheduling or refunds as per guidelines provided by relevant authorities.

We monitor travel advisories and proactively communicate with affected customers.
