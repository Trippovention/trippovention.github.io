Detailed Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Botswana & Transfer to Maun

  • Arrive at Maun International Airport
  • Meet representative and private transfer to lodge
  • Check-in and refresh
  • Relax amidst African wilderness surroundings
  • Overnight stay at lodge
Day 2

Okavango Delta Safari Experience

  • Breakfast at lodge
  • Transfer to Okavango Delta
  • Enjoy scenic mokoro canoe safari
  • Spot birds, hippos and wildlife along waterways
  • Overnight stay at safari camp
Meals: Breakfast
Tickets: Included
Day 3

Full Day Okavango Wildlife Safari

  • Breakfast at camp
  • Full day game drive safari in delta region
  • Spot elephants, zebras and antelopes
  • Enjoy scenic photography and sunset views
  • Overnight stay at safari camp
Meals: Breakfast
Tickets: Included
Day 4

Okavango to Chobe National Park

  • Breakfast at camp
  • Scenic transfer to Chobe National Park
  • Check-in to safari lodge
  • Evening wildlife safari drive
  • Overnight stay at lodge
Meals: Breakfast
Tickets: Included
Day 5

Chobe Safari & River Cruise

  • Breakfast at lodge
  • Morning Jeep Safari in Chobe National Park
  • Spot lions, buffaloes and giraffes
  • Evening Chobe River sunset cruise
  • Overnight stay at lodge
Meals: Breakfast
Tickets: Included
Day 6

Leisure & Nature Experience

  • Overnight stay at lodge
