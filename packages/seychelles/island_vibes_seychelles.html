Detailed Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Seychelles & Transfer to Mahé

  • Arrive at Seychelles International Airport
  • Private transfer to beach resort on Mahé
  • Check-in and refresh
  • Relax by beach or poolside
  • Evening group dinner with island vibes
Day 2

Mahé Sightseeing Tour

  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Visit Victoria city highlights
  • Explore Botanical Garden
  • Visit scenic beaches and viewpoints
  • Return to hotel for overnight stay
Meals: Breakfast
Tickets: Included
Day 3

Praslin & La Digue Adventure

  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Full day excursion to Praslin and La Digue
  • Visit Vallée de Mai Nature Reserve
  • Explore Anse Source d’Argent Beach
  • Return to Mahé for overnight stay
Meals: Breakfast
Tickets: Included

Leisure & Water Activities

  • Morning free at leisure by beach or pool
  • Optional snorkeling / kayaking / paddleboarding
  • Relax with friends at seaside cafés
  • Overnight stay at resort
Meals: Breakfast
Day 5

Sunset Cruise & Beach Fun

  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Free time for beach relaxation
  • Enjoy sunset catamaran cruise
  • Music, scenic views and group fun onboard
  • Return for overnight stay
Meals: Breakfast
Tickets: Included
Day 6

Departure

  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Hotel check-out
  • Private transfer to airport
  • Departure with amazing group memories
  • End of your island holiday
