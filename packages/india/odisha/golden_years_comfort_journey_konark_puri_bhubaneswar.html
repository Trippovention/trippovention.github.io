Detailed Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Bhubaneswar

  • Easy City Tour & Temple Darshan
  • Arrive at Bhubaneswar Airport & private transfer to hotel
  • Check-in & relax after the journey
  • Visit Lingaraj Temple (outside darshan if entry restricted)
  • Explore Mukteshwar Temple & nearby surroundings
Meals: As per itinerary
Day 2

Bhubaneswar Heritage & Caves

  • Khandagiri & Udayagiri Caves
  • Visit Dhauli Shanti Stupa (peaceful Buddhist site)
  • Explore Udayagiri & Khandagiri Caves (easy viewpoints for seniors)
  • Visit Rajarani Temple
  • Evening visit to Ekamra Haat for handicraft shopping
Meals: Breakfast
Tickets: Included
Day 3

Bhubaneswar to Puri

  • Jagannath Temple & Golden Beach
  • Drive from Bhubaneswar to Puri
  • Visit Jagannath Temple Puri Darshan (senior-friendly darshan timings)
  • Check-in at hotel and rest
  • Evening leisurely walk at Puri Golden Beach
Meals: Breakfast
Day 4

Puri to Konark

  • Konark Sun Temple & Chandrabhaga Beach
  • Drive to Konark from Puri
  • Visit Konark Sun Temple (UNESCO World Heritage Site)
  • Explore stone carvings & wheel structures
  • Visit Chandrabhaga Beach and return to Puri
Meals: Breakfast
Tickets: Included
Day 5

Chilika Lake Excursion

  • Satapada Dolphins & Backwater Views
  • Visit Chilika Lake Satapada
  • Enjoy scenic boat ride with dolphin spotting
  • Visit Nalaban Bird Sanctuary viewpoint (optional)
  • Return to Puri for evening leisure
Meals: Breakfast
Tickets: Included
Day 6

Departure

  • Goodbye Odisha
  • Free time for packing or short local walk
  • Hotel check-out
  • Transfer to Bhubaneswar Airport
  • Departure with peaceful spiritual memories
Meals: Breakfast
